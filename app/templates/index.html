ATP is a great way to analysis business process event log.

Dataset  You can upload and browser your dataset here. Your dataset must be csv format, and the first column must be target variable.And we assume that you have preprocessed your dataset such as text features vectoring, Imputation of missing values, normalization and so on.

Topic You can browser your topics here.Especially you can browser and compare predictable models here, choose one to make a predict online.
