ATP is a good way to analyse business process event log.

Dataset  You can upload and browser your dataset here. Your dataset must be csv format, and the first column must be target variable.And we assume that you have preprocessed your dataset such as text features vectoring, Imputation of missing values, normalization and so on.

Feature You can analyse the target dataset here, including its describe and featurs' Correlation with target variable.What's more, you could select interested features to build a predictable model to use later.

Subject You can browse your subjects here.Especially you can browse and compare predictable models within it, choose one to make a predict online.

Note All the online functions have good designed API, you can easily integrated into your information system.
